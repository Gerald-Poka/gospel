In The Vision Intensified, Demos predicted that the original Vision he saw (and re-tells in this booklet) “will intensify in an increasingly powerful wave of His glory!” Demos also implores his readers to re-focus on that original call, especially as many believers sense, as Demos did, that we are in the end-times. In both his books, Demos emphasized that the Vision was not for a big, centralized organization. As the writer says in ‘The Happiest People on Earth’, “the Spirit of God conveyed to Demos the message, ‘I do not want a big centralized organization… of course, come together on occasion to inspire and encourage each other, to light a fire bright enough for many to see. But for your day-in, year-out work, I want you [to be] small, local, sensitive to Me. I do not want uniformity. I will never come to two men or two places in just the same way. Give Me outlets for My infinite variety”

“The Ultimate Dimension”, Demos shares his vision of the future showing how end of time generation will be anointed, prepared and will walk in awesome power to manifest God’s will in the final days before Jesus comes once again; and how the generation will do more than an entire army of ordinary Christian soldiers. The book looks on how God is looking for few dedicated, passionate people whom He can trust and empower to fulfil his plan.

“The happiest people on earth” The book looks through four generations and almost 150 years of the Shakarian family story, showing the direct guidance of the Heavenly Father by the prophetic word. From Demos, Sr., believing God and leaving his homeland of Armenia, in order to follow the prophetic word, to Isaac, his son, finding favor and in the time of great depression, blessed from rags to riches, was given great favor in high government circles and with the great business leaders. To Demos anointed to "call men back to God," and release the Holy Spirit through independent meetings—in neutral locations, thereby igniting the great charismatic renewal. Through a vision Demos founded the Full Gospel Business Men's Fellowship International. To Richard, whose great love of the Lord has guided his life.

As you read this book, I believe that same anointed blessing and revelation will flow into your life. So, join me now as I share with you some of the spiritual truths God has birthed in my spirit. Together, as we keep our eyes fixed squarely upon Jesus, let us come together in one spirit and proclaim,” Come Let Us Exalt Him”

As the Founding President, gathered the FGBMFI leaders from all over the world during the month of November 1982 on the 30th Anniversary of the Fellowship. During that meeting, Demos laid out the prophetic direction from God for the future of the Fellowship and for every member. God had used FGBMFI to spark the Charismatic Renewal that brought the power to witness of Jesus to over 100 million Christians in little more than a decade. But now, Jesus was making Demos aware of something greater– an End time strategy - to change the body of Christ and prepare her for participating in “The Greatest Business in the World” - the Great Harvest!

“Divine life” was birthed out of continual prayer, long-term fasting, adversity, and sever attacks in Demos Shakarian’s life. After God’s reply, he began to walk in God’s power and His authority in His divine life. The book describes the awesome personal relationship between God of the Universe with us and His intent on us to use His spiritual gifts in these final hours of spiritual history.

The book “End Time Vision” follows a stunning vision given to American preacher Tommy Hicks who was a major figure in the powerful 1954 Argentina Revival. “Vision of the Body of Christ and the End-Time Ministries”.
